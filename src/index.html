About 3toe

Hey, thanks for being reading this, you are discovering 3toe, the first resilent humor memecoin.

It all began in 2021 when I was riding my motorcycle back home and had an unfortunate collision with a car. My left foot was crushed by the car that hit me, breaking all five toes on my left foot. After a few weeks and two surgeries, I was finally able to return home with two fewer toes, to continue my recovery. After several physical therapy sessions and months of effort and patience, I was able to walk normally again.

My mission with 3toe is to bring people together through laughter while supporting each other as a community, raising awareness about our insecurities.

Whether you’re here for the laughs, the community, the generational wealth or the deeper message, i welcome you to join the 3toe family. Together, let’s celebrate the lighthearted side of life’s challenges.
